# Thesis Structure Reference

## Romanian Psychology Bachelor Thesis Format

Standard structure following Romanian university conventions:

### Front Matter
1. **CopertÄƒ** (Cover page)
   - University name
   - Faculty and department
   - Program of study
   - Thesis title
   - Scientific coordinator
   - Student name
   - City and date

2. **Cuprins** (Table of Contents)
   - All chapters with page numbers
   - List of tables
   - List of figures

3. **Rezumat** (Abstract)
   - 150-300 words
   - Romanian and optionally English

### Main Body

4. **Introducere** (Introduction)
   - 2-4 pages
   - Research context, relevance, objectives

5. **Capitolul 1: Cadru Teoretic** (Theoretical Framework)
   - 15-25 pages
   - Literature review organized by construct
   - Subcapitole for each major concept

6. **Capitolul 2: Metodologie** (Methodology)
   - 3-5 pages
   - Research methods overview
   - Instrument descriptions

7. **Capitolul 3: Rezultate** (Results)
   - 10-15 pages
   - Sample description
   - Descriptive statistics
   - Hypothesis testing

8. **Capitolul 4: Concluzii** (Conclusions)
   - 3-5 pages
   - Discussion of findings
   - Implications and limitations

### Back Matter

9. **Bibliografie** (References)
   - APA 7th edition format
   - Alphabetical order

10. **Anexe** (Appendices)
    - Full instrument items
    - Additional tables/figures

## Formatting Standards

### Page Layout
- A4 paper
- Margins: 2.5 cm all sides (or 3 cm left for binding)
- Font: Times New Roman 12pt
- Line spacing: 1.5
- Paragraph indent: 1.25 cm
- Page numbers: bottom center

### Headings
- Chapter titles: Bold, 14pt, centered
- Section headings: Bold, 12pt, left-aligned
- Subsection headings: Bold italic, 12pt, left-aligned

### Tables and Figures
- Numbered per chapter (Table 3.1, Figure 3.2)
- Title above tables, below figures
- Source attribution below

## Section Length Guidelines

| Section | Pages | Words (approx) |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| Abstract | 0.5-1 | 150-300 |
| Introduction | 2-4 | 500-1000 |
| Chapter 1 (Theory) | 15-25 | 4000-6500 |
| Chapter 2 (Methods) | 3-5 | 750-1250 |
| Chapter 3 (Results) | 10-15 | 2500-4000 |
| Chapter 4 (Conclusions) | 3-5 | 750-1250 |
| **Total (excl. refs)** | **35-55** | **8500-14000** |
